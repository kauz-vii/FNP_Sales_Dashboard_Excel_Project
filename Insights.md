# 📊 FNP Sales Dashboard – Insights

## 🔍 Key Insights & Business Impact

---

### 💰 1. Revenue is Highly Occasion-Driven
**Insight:**
- Highest revenue from Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, Holi  
- Lower revenue during non-occasion periods  

**Business Impact:**
- Plan seasonal campaigns  
- Run ads before peak events  
- Optimize inventory for festivals  

---

### 📅 2. Strong Seasonal Sales Pattern
**Insight:**
- Revenue spikes in Feb, March, August, November  

**Business Impact:**
- Predict demand  
- Allocate marketing budget efficiently  
- Prepare supply chain  

---

### 🛍️ 3. Top Products Drive Majority Revenue
**Insight:**
- Top 5 products contribute major revenue  

**Business Impact:**
- Focus on high-performing products  
- Ensure stock availability  
- Use upselling & bundling  

---

### 🎁 4. Category Performance Insight
**Insight:**
- Soft Toys highest  
- Mugs lowest  

**Business Impact:**
- Promote strong categories  
- Improve weak categories  
- Optimize product portfolio  

---

### 🧍 5. Avg Customer Spending ≈ ₹3500
**Insight:**
- Moderate spending per order  

**Business Impact:**
- Set pricing strategy  
- Introduce bundle offers  
- Increase AOV  

---

### 🚚 6. Delivery Time ≈ 5–6 Days
**Insight:**
- Relatively high delivery time  

**Business Impact:**
- Improve logistics  
- Introduce express delivery  
- Increase customer satisfaction  

---

### 🔗 7. Weak Correlation (0.35)
**Insight:**
- Quantity slightly affects delivery time  

**Business Impact:**
- Logistics is stable  
- Safe to promote bulk orders  

---

### 🌍 8. City-wise Concentration
**Insight:**
- Few cities dominate orders  

**Business Impact:**
- Focus marketing geographically  
- Improve logistics in top cities  
- Expand in low-performing regions  

---

### ⏰ 9. Peak Ordering Time
**Insight:**
- Evening & early morning peaks  

**Business Impact:**
- Send notifications at peak hours  
- Run time-based offers  

---

### 📆 10. Day-wise Trends
**Insight:**
- Higher sales on Tuesday & Sunday  

**Business Impact:**
- Launch campaigns on peak days  
- Offer weekend deals  

---

### 🎉 11. Occasion-based Product Popularity
**Insight:**
- Different products perform per occasion  

**Business Impact:**
- Personalized recommendations  
- Improve conversion rates  

---

### 📊 12. Revenue Concentration Risk
**Insight:**
- Revenue depends on few segments  

**Business Impact:**
- Diversify products & markets  
- Reduce dependency risk  

---

### 📈 13. Revenue Volatility
**Insight:**
- Fluctuating monthly revenue  

**Business Impact:**
- Introduce steady revenue streams  
- Corporate & subscription gifting  

---

### 🧠 14. Event-driven Business Model
**Insight:**
- Sales driven by occasions  

**Business Impact:**
- Build reminder systems  
- Push notifications for events  

---

### 💰 15. Upselling Opportunity
**Insight:**
- Moderate AOV  

**Business Impact:**
- Bundle offers  
- Add-ons & combos  
- Increase revenue per customer  

---

## 🚀 Final Summary

The analysis highlights that FNP's business is heavily occasion-driven, with revenue concentrated across specific products, categories, and cities. Strategic improvements in delivery, marketing timing, and product focus can significantly enhance performance and customer satisfaction.
